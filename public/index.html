CODE & CO

A build-first, project-driven coding club at SSIT where students learn by shipping real software. We bypass dry theory to focus on team collaborations, hackathons, open source contributions, and peer-led product cycles.

Join the Club How We Work

How We Work

We grow by building products that people use.

Cohort-Based Cycles

Commit to 6-week product cycles. Form teams, brainstorm, scope features, and design real-world apps with clear ship dates.

Weekly Sprints

Stay accountable with check-ins, resolve blockers together, and participate in weekend hacking sessions.

Senior Mentorship

Gain code reviews, architecture feedback, and career guidance from experienced seniors and alumni.

What We'll Do

CODE & CO isn't a lecture club — it's a build club. Here's what you'll actually be doing.

Weekly Build Sprints

Small teams pick a real problem and ship a working project in a fixed cycle, every week.

Hackathons

Team up for internal hackathons and get

Open Source Contributions

contributing and getting PRs merged into real open source projects.

Peer-Led Workshops

Members teach members — FastAPI, web dev, AI/ML, cybersecurity basics, whatever the club is building with.

Project Showcases

End-of-cycle demo days where teams present what they shipped to the whole club.

Doubt-Wall / Support

An always-open space where members post problems/doubts and get help from peers.
